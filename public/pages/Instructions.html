Application - Instructions

Stage 1: Online Application Submission This step creates your initial record and generates your application reference number.
Data Entry: Complete every required field in the form below. Pay special attention to the spelling of your name (must match official documents) and the accuracy of your contact information (email and mobile number).
Review and Submit: Before clicking the Next button, double-check all entered data. Once submitted, changes cannot be made online.
Confirmation & Reference Number: Upon successful submission, a form will appear immediately displaying your unique Application Reference Number (ARN).
‚≠ê Action Required: You must save this ARN. It will be the only identifier used for your file. An email containing this ARN and a summary of your submission will also be sent to your registered email address.

Stage 2: Scheduling the Verification Appointment This stage is handled by the Admissions Office to coordinate your in-person visit.
Wait for Contact: Within 1-2 working days of submitting your online form, a representative from the Admissions Office will call you on the mobile number provided in Stage 1.
Purpose of the Call: The call is strictly to confirm your basic details and assign a specific Date and Time for your document verification at the school office.
Missing Call Protocol: If you have not received a call after the specified timeframe, please contact the Admissions Office directly at and quote your Application Reference Number.

Stage 3: Mandatory In-Office Document Verification Your application is considered incomplete until this final, critical step is successfully finished.
Preparation: On the day of your appointment, ensure you arrive at the School's office 10 minutes before your scheduled time.
Required Documentation: For verification, you MUST present the ORIGINALS of the following documents:
Your Application Reference Number (ARN).
Applicant's Birth Certificate (Original - Optional).
Previous Academic Report Card/Transcripts (Original).
National Identity Card e.g., UIDAI AADHAR Card (Original).
Verification Process: The Verification Officer will meticulously compare the details and documents presented in person against the data you submitted online in Stage 1.
Outcome: Only applications that pass this verification stage will proceed to the next phase of the school's registration process.
